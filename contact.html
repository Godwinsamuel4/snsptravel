Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your office hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Saturday appointments can be scheduled in advance.

How can I track my visa application?

Contact us via WhatsApp or phone with your reference number, and we'll provide you with the current status of your application.

Do you offer emergency travel assistance?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency support for urgent travel needs. Contact us immediately if you need assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and all major payment platforms including mobile money and card payments.
